Concert Information


STL Music's 2023 Spring Concert, Swing into Spring, is taking place on May 4, 2023 in the STL Gymnasium at 7PM (doors open at 6:30PM).


Tickets are priced at $10 per person.

Tickets will be sold at STL starting April 24, 2023 during all three lunches, or visit Ms. Doria in the music room (room 113).


Please visit @stl_musicprogram and @stlarts for more information and updates!


Performances by:

  1. Junior Band
  2. Grade 10-12 Band
  3. Small Ensembles
  4. Vocal Ensemble
  5. STL Strings
